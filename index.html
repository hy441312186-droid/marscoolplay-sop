1
登录 marscoolplay.com 选择产品

可随时下单，周末统一处理
主播
2
结账时输入专属折扣码
在折扣码栏输入批发折扣码，确认金额正确后继续。

主播
3
填写收货信息并提交订单
填写收货地址和联系电话，选择银行转账付款，提交订单并记录订单编号。
订单编号格式：#XXXX
主播
4
截图订单，发 WhatsApp 通知 Bailin
截取订单确认页截图，发至主播 WhatsApp 群通知运营处理。

主播
STEP 02运营确认订单Bailin 执行
1
登录 Shopify 核对订单信息
收到通知后登录 Shopify 后台，核对产品、数量、折扣码、收货地址是否正确。
处理时限：4 小时内｜有误立即通知主播修改
Bailin
2
通知 Gani 进行仓库审核
核对无误后，WhatsApp 群通知 Gani 审核库存，备注订单编号和需求到货时间。
不接受口头修改，所有修改须主播重新提交
Bailin
STEP 03仓库审核库存Gani 执行
1
核实库存是否充足
在 Shopify 后台确认订单内每个 SKU 库存，判断是否可按时发货。
审核时限：2 小时内｜不足须立即反馈 Bailin
Gani
2
反馈审核结果给 Bailin
库存充足 → 回复「✓ 库存确认，可发货」，推进至 Marcia 审批。库存不足 → 通知 Bailin 联系主播处理。
充足 → 推进主管审批｜不足 → 返回 Bailin 处理
Gani
STEP 04主管审批Marcia 执行
1
审查订单合规性与折扣权限
确认折扣码使用在权限范围内，订单金额和产品类别符合合作协议，有无异常。
审批时限：4 小时内
Marcia
2
审批通过或驳回
通过 → 回复「✓ 主管审批通过」推进至 Nendi。驳回 → 注明原因，由 Bailin 通知主播。
通过 → 财务确认｜驳回 → 注明原因通知主播
Marcia
STEP 05财务确认与发货Nendi + Gani
1
Nendi：核对银行付款
核对银行账户收款金额与订单一致后，在 Shopify 点击「确认付款」，回复「✓ Nendi 确认收款，#XXXX 可发货」。
付款确认后通知 Gani 安排发货
Nendi
2
Gani：打包发货
按订单打包，录入物流单号至 Shopify，系统自动发送发货通知给主播。
发货时限：财务确认后 1 工作日内
Gani
3
Bailin：发送物流信息
发货后，Bailin 在 WhatsApp 群发送物流单号和预计到货时间。
格式：「#XXXX 已发出｜物流：XXXXXX｜预计到货：X月X日」
Bailin
STEP 06每周结算流程周末执行
Nendi：周六汇总账单

1. Shopify 导出本周全部主播订单
2. 按主播分组核算订货总额
3. 确认已付 / 未付金额
4. 制作每人独立结算账单

结算截止 周日 23:59

2. 有异议须 24 小时内提出
3. 无异议完成银行转账
4. 转账截图发 WhatsApp 给 Nendi
5. Nendi 确认收款完成结算

STEP 01Streamer Ordering ProcessStreamer
1

Streamer
STEP 02Operations Order ConfirmationBailin
1
Log into Shopify to verify order details

2
Notify Gani for warehouse stock check

2
Report result to Bailin

OK → Forward to Marcia · Insufficient → Return to Bailin
Gani
STEP 04Manager ApprovalMarcia
1
Review compliance and discount permissions
Confirm the discount code is within authorized limits, order amount and product category comply with the partnership agreement, and no irregularities exist.
Time limit: within 4 hours of receiving stock confirmation
Marcia
2
Approve or reject
Approved → Reply "✓ Manager approved" and forward to Nendi. Rejected → State reason clearly; Bailin notifies streamer.
Approved → Finance confirmation · Rejected → State reason and notify streamer
Marcia
STEP 05Finance Confirmation & ShipmentNendi + Gani
1
Nendi: Verify bank payment
Verify the bank account has received the correct amount matching the order. Then update Shopify to "Payment confirmed" and reply "✓ Nendi confirmed payment, #XXXX ready to ship."
Notify Gani to arrange shipment after confirming payment
Nendi
2
Gani: Pack and ship
Pack order, enter tracking number into Shopify. System automatically sends shipment notification to streamer.
Time limit: 1 business day after finance confirmation · Tracking number must be entered in Shopify
Gani
3

After shipment, Bailin sends tracking number and estimated delivery date in the streamer WhatsApp group.

STEP 06Weekly Settlement ProcessWeekend
Nendi: Compile Settlement (Saturday)
Every Saturday
1. Export all streamer orders from Shopify
2. Group by streamer and calculate totals
3. Confirm paid / unpaid amounts
4. Create individual settlement statement per streamer
5. Send to each streamer via WhatsApp
Streamer: Complete Payment (Sunday)
Deadline: Sunday 23:59
1. Review settlement statement received
2. Raise disputes within 24 hours if any
3. Complete bank transfer per statement amount
4. Send transfer screenshot to Nendi via WhatsApp
5. Nendi confirms receipt to complete settlement
